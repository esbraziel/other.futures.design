About us

We're a working group designing artifacts (architecture, furniture, relationships, terminology, ideology, narratives, etc.) for different social formations - and what you get when you put a motley crew of dreamers, designers, doers, do-gooders, coders, documentarians, academics, and researchers together to craft other futures. We run as an anarchic, do-ocratic project of the commons, with a heavy bend toward applying critical theory to projects that may not get funded, but are what a utopic future should have. We know the world is changing - and so we get our hands dirty creating the equitable social, political, and economic relations that make 'other worlds' better suited to (post)human life.

Utopic Urbanism(s)
build series

We often have opportunities for lectures and reflection around how the future could be, but few opportunities to build the world we want to live in.

We're is crafting the future we want through themed build days. We have workshopped and are currently prototyping semi-permanent and permanent installations in SF.

Science Fiction
Writing group

Through imagining and producing science fiction that examines its potential as a transformative instrument, the group brings together authors (and aspiring authors), futurists and utopians who would like to exchange ideas, affects and inspiration, and to build off of one another in their generation of science fictional material.
